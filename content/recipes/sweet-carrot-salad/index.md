## Ingredients:
  - Shredded carrot
  - Raisins
  - Optional: handful of inca seeds
  - Optional: roasted pistachios

## Dressing:
  - Garlic (powder or fresh)
  - Ginger
  - (Vegan) mayonnaise
  - Mustard
  - Curry powder
  - Lemon juice
  - Optional: touch of sweetener (agave syrup, maple syrup or honey)
